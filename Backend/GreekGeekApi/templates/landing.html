FAQ

Answers for chapters and organization admins.

What is GreekGeek?

GreekGeek is a study hour tracking app for fraternities, sororities, and campus organizations.

Can members sign up without an organization code?

No. Members need an organization registration code so their account connects to the correct chapter or group.

How does location-based study verification work?

Admins define approved study locations, and members use the mobile app to clock in when they are at those locations.

Can admins set custom study periods?

Yes. GreekGeek supports organization study periods and required hours for tracking member progress.

Is this only for Greek life?

The product is built around Greek organization workflows, but it can also support campus groups with required study hours.

How do we start?
